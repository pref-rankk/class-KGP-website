Introduction

Welcome to the Class KGP Extension Privacy Policy. This policy outlines how we handle your data and maintain your privacy while you use our extension. We are dedicated to ensuring the security of your information and transparency in our data practices. Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with this policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your data. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to providing a secure and user-friendly experience with our extension.

Information We Collect

We exclusively gather necessary data on a temporary basis to enable the smooth operation of the extension. This data, specifically the student timetable obtained from the Student's ERP, is never stored on our servers and is strictly utilized locally for seamless extension functionality.

How We Use Your Information

Permissions Required: The tabs permission allows us to retrieve timetables from the IIT Kharagpur ERP website. The identity permission enables secure authentication for Google Calendar integration.

Purpose: We use your data to automate academic scheduling at IIT Kharagpur, fetching your student ERP timetable, and creating Google Calendar events securely and efficiently.

Sharing Your Information

Disclosure: We do not sell or transfer user data to third parties, except for approved use cases.

Data Retention

No data is retained by the system anywhere. No human can view or edit the user data anytime during or after the execution of the services of this extension.

Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, ensuring confidentiality and security.

User Rights

We do not have any authority over any data that this service requires. The entire authority lies with you at all the times

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies and tracking technologies for providing our services.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
